About this piece

Our Classic Rib Pumpkin is the anchor piece of the HallowCeramics collection. Each one is hand-carved from stoneware clay — the ribs, the stem, the silhouette — before a slow dry, a strength-building bisque fire, and a final glaze that gives the surface a soft, believable depth.

Unlike factory-molded decor, these pumpkins vary slightly from piece to piece. That's intentional: the subtle asymmetry is what makes them read as real, not plastic.
